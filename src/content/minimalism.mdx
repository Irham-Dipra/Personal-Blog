Minimalism isn't just about having less; it's about making room for more of what matters. In web design, this translates to stripping away non-essential elements like excessive colors, complex borders, and overwhelming animations.

## Typography as Design

When you remove color, typography takes center stage. Choosing the right font, managing line height, and ensuring proper contrast become the primary tools for conveying emotion and hierarchy.

* Geist Sans for clean body text
* High contrast for readability
* Ample whitespace to let the content breathe
